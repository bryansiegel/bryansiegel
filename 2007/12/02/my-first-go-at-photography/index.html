My First Go at Photography

Dec 2nd, 2007 | Comments

I’ve been a fan of Photoshop but lacked intersting pics (there’s only so much you can do with family photo’s). So I picked up my hp photosmart 435 piece of crap and decided to go outside and take some pics. Here is the outcome of my Amateur Photography;

Here is my camera on night view. It kind of reminds me of the Perris Hilton sex tape.

I turned the night view off and decided to use the normal flash feature. I have no clue what that light is, but I’m thinking aliens.


Since I’m a glutton for punishment I decided to try out the night view feature again.


Same pic minus the night view (or make your picture green) effect. Well…. my pics look like crap with or without the effect. If you thought that was all of the punishment I was going to put you through you thought wrong. Hahaha, ha.


It took some serious skills (Beer) to get this picture of a tree to look like this.


Ahhh! The Aliens are back!

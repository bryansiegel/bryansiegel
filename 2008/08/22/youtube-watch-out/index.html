YouTube Watch Out

Aug 22nd, 2008 | Comments

As the Internet gets smarter and Internet speeds get faster my web experience is getting a whole lot better. I remember dial up speeds and the bright colors of Internets past. I remember the first time I saw YouTube and thinking to myself, “this is where things are going to lead.” Successful websites are all about the experience and ease of use. There are 2 video websites I visit, when I need a break..

FunnyOrDie.com is hands down the great idea of the year. A social funny video site mixed in with a YouTube embed feature leaves me to wonder how long YouTube will stay ahead of the video game. Check Will Ferrell in the most funniest video I’ve seen on the site so far;

See more Will Ferrell videos at Funny or Die


Hulu.com - Is the legal way of watching T.V. shows and movies. They have a large selection to choose from. I’ve seen a few videos such as Firefly and The Dresden Files there along with web clips which can be short documentaries of actors or full blown advertisements of shows to come.
